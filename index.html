Create Change

Founded in 2019, Create Change is a non-governmental organization which aims to support creative industries through research, innovation and development initiatives, contributing to the growth of local communities and individuals.

We're involved in

  • increasing cohesion and collaboration between and within technical communities
  • supporting and promote creative industries by research, development and innovation
  • development of technical and digital skills through trainings, workshops, meetups
  • supporting entrepreneurship by improving skills of those working in creative industries
