Donate 2% of your income tax

If you pay Romanian income tax, the current laws gives you the opportunity to direct 2% of your income tax towards Asociația Create Change.

Steps to donate 2% of your income tax

  1. 1. Download form 230 pre-filled with the details of Asociația Create Change
  2. 2. Fill in your personal details
  3. 3. Deliver the completed form to your local ANAF office by post or in person by May 22nd, 2022.
  • Download form 230
  • Fill form on Redirectioneaza.ro

Donate 20% of your income tax

The Sponsorship Law and the Tax Code allow companies subject to profits tax to grant sponsorships to organizations from certain areas and to benefit from tax incentives in certain conditions provided by the law.

The expense made with sponsorships is not a tax deductible expense, but it may represent tax credit, that is, it may reduce the taxable profit payable with the full amount of sponsorship offered.
